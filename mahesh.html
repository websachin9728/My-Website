Marvel Studios Characters


Iron Man

Tony Stark is a genius inventor and billionaire industrialist who uses a powered suit of armor to protect the world as Iron Man.

Captain America

Steve Rogers was transformed into a super-soldier known as Captain America during World War II, and he continues to fight for American ideals and as a leader of the Avengers.

Thor

Thor is the Asgardian god of thunder and a member of the Avengers, using his powerful hammer Mjolnir to defend Earth from threats both earthly and cosmic.

The Hulk

Dr. Bruce Banner transforms into the massive green rage monster known as the Hulk when he becomes angry or stressed, using his immense strength to battle threats to the planet.

Black Widow

Natasha Romanoff is a highly skilled spy and assassin who has defected from her former employer, and she it one of the best and skilled agent of the shield.

Spider-Man

Peter Parker is a high school student who gained spider-like abilities after being bitten by a radioactive spider, and he uses his powers to protect New York City as Spider-Man.

Doctor Strange

Dr. Stephen Strange was a brilliant surgeon whose career was ended after a car accident, but he later became the Sorcerer Supreme and uses his magic to protect the world from mystical threats.

Black Panther

T'Challa is the king of the fictional African nation of Wakanda, and he dons the mantle of the Black Panther to protect his people and the world from threats.

Ant-Man

Scott Lang is a former thief who becomes the superhero Ant-Man, able to shrink in size and control ants with the help of a high-tech suit.

Wasp

Scott Lang is a former thief who becomes the superhero Ant-Man, able to shrink in size and control ants with the help of a high-tech suit.

Scarlet Witch

Wanda Maximoff is a mutant with reality-warping abilities, able to manipulate energy, control minds, and alter reality.

Vision

An android created by Ultron using the mind stone, Vision possesses superhuman strength, density control, and the ability to shoot energy beams from his forehead.
